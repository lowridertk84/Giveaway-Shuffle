Overview

Giveaway Shuffle is a Discord bot operated by Black Wolf Union. This Privacy Policy explains what information the bot stores, how it is used, and how users may request or remove data.

Information Collected

Giveaway Shuffle may store the following information when used in a Discord server:

  • Discord server IDs
  • Discord channel IDs
  • Discord user IDs for giveaway creators or command users
  • Giveaway names and descriptions
  • Contestant names entered into giveaways
  • Entry counts
  • Giveaway closing and drawing times
  • Winner results
  • Basic error logs needed for troubleshooting

How Information Is Used

This information is used only to operate Giveaway Shuffle, including:

  • Creating and managing giveaways
  • Tracking entries
  • Updating giveaway posts
  • Closing giveaways at scheduled times
  • Selecting and announcing winners
  • Troubleshooting technical issues

Contestant Names

Contestant names are entered manually by giveaway hosts. These names are stored only for the purpose of running and displaying giveaway entries.

Data Sharing

Giveaway Shuffle does not sell, rent, trade, or monetize user data. Data is not shared with third parties except as required to operate through Discord or as required by law.

Data Deletion

Giveaway hosts can delete contests they created using bot commands. Deleting a giveaway removes the associated giveaway information and entries from active bot storage.

Server owners or administrators may also request removal of stored giveaway data by contacting the bot operator.

Storage and Security

Giveaway Shuffle data is stored on privately managed infrastructure operated by the bot owner. Reasonable steps are taken to protect stored data, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

Giveaway Shuffle operates on Discord and relies on Discord’s platform to receive commands, display messages, and manage server interactions. Use of Discord is subject to Discord’s own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Project information and policy pages may be hosted through GitHub Pages. Optional donations may be processed through Ko-fi or other third-party payment platforms if linked.

Children’s Privacy

Giveaway Shuffle is intended for use within Discord servers and does not knowingly collect information from children outside of normal Discord bot operation.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of Giveaway Shuffle after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions or data removal requests can be directed to Black Wolf Union through the official Giveaway Shuffle support channel or project page.
