How Giveaway Shuffle Works

Giveaway Shuffle uses a two-stage drawing system designed to reward higher entry counts while keeping the final reveal exciting and fair.

Stage 1: Ticket Pool Selection

Every entry acts as its own ticket.

  • Blue = 5 tickets
  • Red = 3 tickets
  • Green = 2 tickets

The more tickets a contestant has, the better their odds of reaching the Top 10 finalists.

Stage 2: Top 10 Finalist Round

Once the Top 10 finalists are selected, each finalist is represented only once regardless of how many original tickets they had.

The final elimination round is then based on equal single-entry odds for all Top 10 finalists.

Why This System?

  • More entries matter during the first stage.
  • Finalists all get an equal shot to win.
  • Creates suspense and fairness.

Closing & Drawing Times

Giveaway Shuffle allows separate control for entry closing time and winner drawing time.

  • Close entries and draw immediately
  • Close entries first, draw later
  • Perfect for live streams and scheduled reveals
